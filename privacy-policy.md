# Privacy Policy

## 1. General Information

This Privacy Policy describes how the Sea Battle application ("the App") handles user data.

The App is developed for educational purposes and does not aim to collect or process personal data for commercial use.

---

## 2. Data Collection

The App does **not intentionally collect** personal data such as:
- Name
- Email
- Phone number
- Location
- Payment information

However, basic technical data may be processed automatically by the browser:
- Browser type
- Device type
- Local storage data (if enabled in the game)

---

## 3. Cookies and Local Storage

The App may use:
- Local Storage (to save game state, settings, preferences)
- Cookies (only if cookie consent is implemented)

This data is stored locally in the user's browser and is not transmitted to external servers.

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

The App does not use third-party analytics or advertising services by default.

If any external libraries are added (e.g. for UI, cookies, or analytics), they may have their own privacy policies.

---

## 5. GDPR Compliance

In accordance with GDPR principles:
- Users are informed about any data storage (e.g. cookies/local storage)
- Users have the right to clear stored data by clearing browser storage
- No personal data is sold or shared with third parties

---

## 6. Data Deletion

Users can delete all locally stored data by:
- Clearing browser cache
- Removing site data from browser settings

---

## 7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated during development. All changes will be reflected in this document.

---

## 8. Contact

This project is developed for educational purposes.  
For questions, contact the developer via project repository.
